Choco Milky Sales Insights with Tableau

Choco Milky, a Belgium-based chocolate company operating across six European countries, struggled with underperforming regions, inconsistent delivery performance, and limited visibility into sales and customer behavior. Using Tableau, a consolidated dashboard was developed to provide a unified view of sales performance, delivery trends, and product profitability.
